SEM TRAINING CERTIFICATIONS

Overview

This training covers the fundamental operation of the FEI SEM tool, focusing on essential system components, software navigation, and proper usage. Mastering these basics ensures accurate imaging, prevents equipment damage, and optimizes performance for analytical applications.

Training Tasks

  • Identify key components: Column, Detectors, Vacuum, Stage, GIS
  • Perform startup and shutdown
  • Operate vacuum system
  • Navigate SEM software
  • Control Stage for positioning
  • Adjust beam setting (voltage, current, distance)
  • Follow safety protocols
